üîç What Are QR Codes and Why They Matter

QR (Quick Response) codes are two-dimensional barcodes that store data. Originally developed in Japan for automotive manufacturing, they are now widely used in marketing, payments, identity verification, and more. What makes QR codes powerful is their ability to instantly convey data with a quick scan using a smartphone camera. With the rise of contactless technology and mobile-first design, QR codes have become essential tools for businesses and developers alike.
